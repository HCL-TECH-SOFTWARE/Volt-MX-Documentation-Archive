Keyboard Shortcuts

The following keyboard shortcuts are available in VoltMX Iris.

Mac Key Windows Key Function
Control + Click Right-click Opens a widget’s context menu.
<p> ⌘ + - </p> <p> Ctrl + - </p> <p> Decreases the Code Editor font size and becomes the new preferred size in the Preferences dialog box. </p>
<p> ⌘ + [ </p> <p> Ctrl + [ </p> <p> Moves the selection down one layer in the z-index. </p>
<p> ⌘ + ] </p> <p> Ctrl + ] </p> <p> Moves the selection up one layer in the z-index. </p>
<p> ⌘ + = </p> <p> Ctrl + = </p> <p> Increases the Code Editor font size and becomes the new preferred size in the Preferences dialog box. </p>
⌘ + ; Ctrl + ; Toggles the horizontal and vertical rulers on and off when working on a Desktop channel form on the Iris Canvas.
<p> ⌘ + A </p> <p> Ctrl + A </p> <p> In the context of a selected form or flex container, selects all the child widgets. </p>
<p> ⌘ + A </p> <p> Ctrl + A </p> <p> In the context of the Code Editor, selects all code. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Alt + O </p> <p> Ctrl + Alt + O </p> <p> Opens the Resources folder. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Alt + T </p> <p> Ctrl + Alt + T </p> <p> Opens the Build folder. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Alt + W R </p> <p> Ctrl + Alt + W R </p> <p> Opens the WebApp Native folder (applicable only in HCL Iris Classic). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Alt + W T </p> <p> Ctrl + Alt + W T </p> <p> Opens the WebApp Mobile App folder. </p>
<p> ⌘ + B </p> <p> Ctrl + B </p> <p>Initiates the Build Last command, which uses the most recently-selected platform(s) and channel(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + C </p> <p> Ctrl + C </p> <p> Copies the selected widget(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + C </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + C </p> <p> Copies the selected widget’s skin. </p>
<p> ⌘ + D </p> <p> Ctrl + D </p> <p> Duplicates the selected widget(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Delete </p> <p> Delete </p> <p> Deletes the selected widget(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Drag </p> <p> N/A </p> <p> Constrains the movement of the selected widgets to either the horizontal or vertical axis. </p>
<p> ⌘ + F </p> <p> Ctrl + F </p> <p> When the Code Editor is active, opens the Code Editor Find and Replace feature. In other contexts, opens the project Search text box in the Project Explorer. </p>
F2 F2 <p>When used, invokes the rename function. User can modify the name of the asset to a new name. The rename shortcut supprts the following assets:</p> <ul> <li>Widgets (including forked forms, masters, notifications, glances)</li> <li>Skins</li> <li>Components</li> <li>Modules</li> <li>MVC Extensions</li> <li>Web apps (including preview)</li> <li>Test resources</li> <li>Named actions</li> <li>User widget modules</li> <li>App extension JS files</li> </ul> <p>Renaming a splash screen, app events, controller files, custom resources, model files, external files, and read-only files is not supported.</p>
<p> ⌘ + Alt + F </p> <p> Ctrl + Alt + F </p> <p>Opens the project Search text box in the Project Explorer. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + F </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + F </p> <p>Opens the Search tab in the Console for conducting global searches. </p>
<p> ⌘ + H </p> <p> Ctrl + H </p> <p> Hides the selected widgets. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + H </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + H </p> <p> Unhides the selected widgets. </p>
Ctrl + L Ctrl + L When used in the context of the Code Editor, opens a command bar for jumping to a specific line number.
<p> ⌘ + L </p> <p> Ctrl + L </p> <p> When used in the context of the Iris Canvas, locks the selected widgets. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + L </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + L </p> <p> Unlocks the selected widgets. </p>
<p> ⌘ + O </p> <p> Ctrl +O </p> <p> Used within an open JavaScript file in the Code Editor. Displays a list of the code elements within the JavaScript file, including objects, functions, arrays, numbers, and strings. Double-clicking a code element in the list navigates to that code element.</p>
Alt + Q Ctrl + Q Navigates to the previously edited module.
<p> ⌘ + R </p> <p> Ctrl + R </p> <p> Initiates the Run Last command, building a functional preview of the app using the most recently-used platform and channel selections. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + R </p> <p> Ctrl +Shift + R </p> <p> Initiates the Run with the VoltMX Iris. Events command, building a functional preview using the Developer events mode. </p>
<p> ⌘ + S </p> <p> Ctrl + S </p> <p> Saves the currently selected project entity (e.g. form). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + S </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + S </p> <p> Saves the current project. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + < </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + < </p> <p> Decreases the font size of the selected widget(s) by 10 units </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + > </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + > </p> <p> Increases the font size of the selected widget(s) by 10 units </p>
<p> ⌘ + U </p> <p> Ctrl + U </p> <p> Un-parents the selected widget from its parent container, making it a child to the parent of the container it was un-parented from. </p>
<p> ⌘ + V </p> <p> Ctrl + V </p> <p> Pastes the copied widget(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + V </p> <p> Ctrl + Shift + V </p> <p> Pastes the skin that is on the clipboard to the selected widget. </p>
<p> ⌘ + W </p> <p> Ctrl + W </p> <p> Closes the item currently open on the Iris Canvas. </p>
<p> ⌘ + X </p> <p> Ctrl + X </p> <p> Cuts the selected widget(s). </p>
<p> ⌘ + Shift + Z </p> <p> Ctrl + Y </p> <p> Repeats the last action. </p>
<p> ⌘ + Z </p> <p> Ctrl + Z </p> <p> Undoes the last action. </p>
Alt + Drag Alt + Drag Changes the Snap mode to function to opposite of what is configured.
<p> Down Arrow </p> <p> Down Arrow </p> <p> Moves the selected item(s) down by one unit. </p>
<p> Esc </p> <p> Esc </p> <p> Depending on the context, the Esc key cancels the current move or resize action on the Iris Canvas, or closes the search panel (if open) in the Code Editor window. </p>
<p> Left Arrow </p> <p> Left Arrow </p> <p> Moves the selected item(s) to the left by one unit. </p>
<p> Option + ‘ </p> <p> Alt + ‘ </p> <p> Selects the right sibling. </p>
<p> Option + ; </p> <p> Alt + ; </p> <p> Selects the left sibling. </p>
<p> Option + [ </p> <p> Alt + [ </p> <p> Selects the parent container. </p>
<p> Option + ] </p> <p> Alt + ] </p> <p> Selects the first child widget. </p>
<p> Option + Drag </p> <p> Ctrl + Drag </p> <p> Disables grid snapping, widget snapping, and widget guides while moving or dragging the selection on the Iris Canvas. </p>
<p> Option + N </p> <p> Alt + N </p> <p> Creates a new project. </p>
<p> Option + Shift + V </p> <p> Alt + Shift + V </p> <p> Assigns the skin in the clipboard to the selected widget. </p>
<p> Right Arrow </p> <p> Right Arrow </p> <p> Moves the selected item(s) to the right by one unit. </p>
<p> Shift + Arrow </p> <p> Shift + Arrow </p> <p> Moves the selected item(s) in the direction of the arrow by 10 units.</p>
<p> Shift + Drag </p> <p> Shift + Drag </p> <p> Disables grid snapping, widget snapping, and widget guides while moving or dragging the selection along the given axis. </p>
<p> Shift + Resize </p> <p> Shift + Resize </p> <p> Constrains the aspect ratio while scaling the selected widget. Applicable only if scaling handles are selected. </p>
Space bar + mouse motion Space bar + mouse motion Moves the canvas around in the BVR mode.
<p> Up Arrow </p> <p> Up Arrow </p> <p> Moves the selected item(s) up by one unit. </p>
